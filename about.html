about me

I’m Benedict, a Nigerian product designer working remotely from Lagos, Nigeria. I am currently a tutor at LuTA in Lagos.

Through a career of designing, I’ve found that I’m at my best when I’m helping others succeed — today, I do that through building design systems for companies or cooperatives. As a designer, I’m passionate about solving tough customer problems by combining thoughtful research, clever technology and beautiful design. I’ve previously contributed to launch of TravalMax.

When I am not designing, I am getting my hands on coding which is what I have been doing for some time now. When I am not designing or coding, I am hanging out with my friends or I am playing chess.
